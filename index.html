Project Overview

Built for a post-merger scenario where three companies (GreenTech, Summit Corp, and Apex Solutions) consolidated into PinHigh, this system addresses the critical challenge of managing employee support requests across legacy systems and policies.

Business Context: During organizational mergers, helpdesk teams face overwhelming ticket volumes related to policy changes, system migrations, and benefit transitions. This solution automates classification, routing, and provides department-specific access control.
